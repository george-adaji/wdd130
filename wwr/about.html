Our mission is to provide safe, thrilling white water rafting experiences that connect people with nature and create lifelong memories.

History

Founded in 1995, our company began with a passion for adventure and a love for wild rivers. What started as a small family operation has grown into one of the region's most trusted rafting outfitters, guiding thousands of adventurers through unforgettable experiences.
