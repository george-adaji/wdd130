Lagos, Nigeria

Lagos is known as no man's land and is one of the most visited cities in Africa. Lagos is the largest city in Nigeria and the fastest growing city in the world. It is located along the Atlantic coast. It is known for its vibrant culture, business opportunities, and energetic atmosphere.

The city is a major financial and cultural hub of Africa, home to beautiful beaches, bustling markets, and a thriving entertainment scene that has made it the heart of Nigeria's film and music industry known as Nollywood. Lagos is my favorite city, and my love for the city is beyond comprehension.
